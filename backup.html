Backup Policy and Documentation

1. Introduction

1.1 Purpose

The purpose of this document is to define the backup strategy, procedures, and guidelines to safeguard the integrity and availability of NovaStream's and their respective client's critical data.

1.2 Scope

This policy covers all systems and data within the NovaStream network, including servers, databases, applications, and user data. Also, this policy may cover third party servers, databases and applications utilized by NovaStream.

2. Backup Strategy

We utilize the 3-2-1-1 rule, which is a data backup strategy designed to ensure the creation of reliable and secure backups for data protection and disaster recovery purposes. The rule provides a straightforward and effective guideline for creating backup copies that are resilient against various risks, including hardware failures, data corruption, and disasters.

  • 3 Copies
    This includes the original data and two additional backup copies. This provides redundancy in case one copy becomes inaccessible or corrupted.
  • 2 Different Media Types
    Data stored on a different type of media to reduce chances of hardware failures or data corruption.
  • 1 Offsite Copy
    This provides protection against events such as natural disasters, theft, or other on-premises incidents that could affect all copies stored in the same physical location.
  • 1 Airgapped Copy
    This copy is kept disconnected from the network when not actively being updated, providing protection against cyber threats and ransomware.

2.1 Backup Storage

2.1.1 Onsite

We securely store your backups on a server located within our office.

2.1.2 Offsite

We utilize DigitalOcean as our offsite storage provider to keep our backups securely offsite.

2.2.3 Cloud

We utilize BackBlaze B2 as our offsite storage provider to keep our backups securely in the cloud.

All files are transmitted using an encrypted TLS connection. We also add our own encryption to your files before transmitting them to Backblaze B2. All transmitted files are stored encrypted at rest inside a private bucket with our own encryption as well as server side encryption provided by BackBlaze.

2.2.4 Airgapped

2.2 Retention Policy

Due to the small size of database (SQL) backups for our client's websites, we may retain these files for longer periods, for additional protection for the customer.

2.1.1 Onsite

Depending on available storage capacity, full backups are also retained on our local storage for up-to 31 days.

2.1.2 Offsite

Latest revision of the full backups are retained on our storage provider for 31 days.

2.2.3 Cloud

Full backups are also retained on our cloud storage for up-to 31 days.

2.2.4 Airgapped

Latest revision of the full backups are retained on airgapped devices for 2 months.
